Jesuit Values

Men and Women for and with others



The idea that individuals should lead from within, not from the front. This is a simple idea that people with the opportunity should ‘teach the man how to fish, rather than give him a fish.’ As stated in my mission statement, I like to bring others’ deas to life, and keep them in the loop when I’m working. This is very important, because those around me in the decision making process, often staying out of it completely, helping produce the solution proposed by others.
