Community Activism

During the first semester community activism project, I was in charge of all the multimedia that was produced, as well as a small social media team. There were people in my team that had previously made social media awareness posts, so I largely allowed them to work independent of myself, and asked them what they needed from me. I produced the elements they needed, as well as a video and a number of custom graphics for the event. I learned a number of project management skills from my position on this committee, the most important of which was the renewed importance of time management skills.


During the second community activism project, I had no role, but still found myself involved, although in a significantly smaller way. I wasn’t given a role, but ended up designing my own social media post for the event because I didn’t like the ones that had been made for the event. I then posted it on my instagram story once per day until the event had ended. I didn’t really learn any new leadership skills from this event.
